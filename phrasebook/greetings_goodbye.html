Greetings – goodbye

As in English, the ways of saying goodbye depend on levels of formality. Here are the ones you need to know:

Arrivederci
Goodbye (polite)
Ciao
Goodbye, See you (familiar)

You might also hear:

ArrivederLa!
Goodbye (polite)

Related phrases:

Ci vediamo!
See you!
A presto!
See you soon!
Alla prossima!
See you soon
Mi raccomando!
Take care!
